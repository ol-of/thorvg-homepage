Why ThorVG

Small engine, wide surface area

The renewed home acts as a hub, but it still preserves the core story: ThorVG is compact, practical, and designed for vector-heavy systems that need portability without excessive complexity.

Structural Design

One binary, modular composition

ThorVG is designed to fit different system shapes while keeping maintenance and binary size under control.

Smart Rendering

Partial updates where possible

Its rendering strategy avoids unnecessary redraws and maps well to animation and embedded UI use cases.

Formats

SVG and Lottie built in

Static vector scenes and animation workflows sit side by side in the core story of the project.

Backends

CPU, OpenGL, WebGL, WebGPU

ThorVG spans software rendering and GPU-backed pipelines to match application and platform constraints.
